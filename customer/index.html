About Us

Abaya Farm is a well-established egg farm in Matara District, Sri Lanka. It was founded over 10 years ago by Mr.Nandapala Abeygunasekara who wanted to produce high-quality eggs and provide excellent service to their customers. Over the years, Our Abaya Farm has gained a reputation for its quality products and dedication to customer satisfaction. We are committed to producing the freshest, highest-quality eggs possible. Our chickens are raised under strict biosecurity measures to ensure their health and well-being. The farm is equipped with modern technology to ensure the best quality products. Abaya Farm is known for its commitment to quality and providing customers with the freshest eggs possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between brown and white eggs?

The color of an eggshell is determined by the breed of chicken that laid the egg, and has no impact on its nutritional value or taste. Brown eggs are laid by chickens with brown feathers, while white eggs are laid by chickens with white feathers.

How long can I keep eggs in the refrigerator?

Eggs can typically be kept in the refrigerator for up to 5 weeks after the "sell-by" date on the carton. However, it's best to use them within 2-3 weeks for optimal freshness.

How do I know if an egg is still good to eat?

To determine if an egg is still good to eat, you can perform the "float test." Fill a bowl with water and place the egg in it. If the egg sinks to the bottom and lays flat, it's still fresh. If it stands upright or floats to the top, it's past its prime and should not be consumed.

Can I eat eggs that have been cracked?

Cracked eggs should not be consumed, as bacteria can enter through the cracks and cause foodborne illness.

What is the nutritional value of an egg?

One large egg contains about 6 grams of protein, 5 grams of fat, and less than 1 gram of carbohydrates. Eggs are also a good source of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin D, choline, and selenium.

Do different chicken breeds produce eggs with different nutritional value?

Different chicken breeds may produce eggs with slightly different nutritional value, but the overall difference is minimal.

What is the best way to store eggs?

Eggs should be stored in a cool, dry place, ideally in a refrigerator. They should be kept in their carton to protect them from moisture and odors.
